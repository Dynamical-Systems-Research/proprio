# Absorbance Plate Read

## Purpose
Measure the absorbance of a sample plate at 600 nm wavelength.

## Steps
1. Reset the instrument to a known state.
2. Open the tray and load the plate.
3. Close the tray.
4. Read the blank (reference) at 600 nm with 100 ms integration time.
5. Read the sample at 600 nm with 100 ms integration time.
6. Open the tray and unload the plate.
7. Shut down the instrument.

## Expected Results
- Blank absorbance ≤ 0.05 AU
- Net sample absorbance (sample − blank) between 0.75 and 0.85 AU
- Detector does not saturate
