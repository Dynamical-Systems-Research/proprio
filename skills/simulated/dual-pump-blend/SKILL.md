---
name: dual-pump-blend
description: Deliver a calibrated 6:4 blend from channels A and B at 60 rpm totalling 10 mL
---

1. Reset the controller.
2. Calibrate channel A to 0.040 mL/revolution.
3. Calibrate channel B to 0.050 mL/revolution.
4. Prime channel A at 60 rpm.
5. Prime channel B at 60 rpm.
6. Pump 6.0 mL from channel A at 60 rpm.
7. Pump 4.0 mL from channel B at 60 rpm.
8. Halt all channels.
