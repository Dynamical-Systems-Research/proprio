# Changelog

All notable changes are documented here.

## 0.1.0 - 2026-07-09

- Composed XRD operation and observability stack with deterministic records.
- Preregistered, independent-engine XRD metrology battery and raw inspection artifacts.
- DSV4 baseline-policy trajectory with honest role labeling.
- Self-verifying Keithley skill acquisition with captured ADMIT and REJECT proofs.
- Frozen cross-family confirmatory panel with six DSV4-authored instrument skills, paired
  truthful/no-feedback repair arms, sealed condition validation, and byte-identical replay.
- Independent 9,000-case verifier metrology battery with zero observed false admissions and
  zero false rejections across optical measurement, calibrated delivery, and thermal control.
- Stateful reviewer calibrated on diagnostic instruments and validated on 24 separate
  confirmatory semantic-mutation cases.
- Public skill packages use `simulation_qualified` status and retain a mandatory separate
  real-hardware qualification gate.
- Simulator-drift evolution stages 8/8 regression-safe proposals with deterministic replay,
  immutable parents, and rollback lineage.
- Expanded replication with ten independent DSV4 generations per confirmatory instrument:
  68/70 initial drafts executed and 64/70 candidates qualified. The original six instruments
  qualified 60/60; the external OpenFlexure family qualified 4/10 and therefore fails the
  frozen four-family generalization gate.
- Pinned OpenFlexure integration through its public API, with a 2,700-case physical-verifier
  battery, an alternate Laplacian focus implementation, zero false-valids, and one false
  rejection among 300 valid cases.
- Measured instrument-specific engineering burden, including adapter, verifier, source-bundle,
  physical-check, and invalid-class counts; person-hours remain explicitly unavailable.
- Independent Qwen 3.7 Plus semantic review with a frozen evidence rubric and deterministic
  gates retained as promotion authority. The original six instruments passed 42/42 cases;
  the full external-family panel matched 47/49 labels and remains `FAIL` after fresh replay
  exposed two unsupported OpenFlexure fixture labels.
- External OpenFlexure drift evolution attempted and rejected fail-closed after target,
  history, reviewer, and locked-validation failures; no microscope skill or proposal entered
  the catalog.
- Hardware-free CI, catalog-bound skills, and a fail-closed MatteriX adapter boundary.
